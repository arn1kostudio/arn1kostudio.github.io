Arn1ko Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Arn1ko Studio handles information for this website and for apps published by Arn1ko Studio, including ArnEQ, Arn Net Scanner, ArnProxy, and Arn dBA Meter. Product-specific pages may add extra details when an app has its own network, account, or device-management behavior.

ArnMesh has its own dedicated Privacy Policy because it uses device pairing, mesh networking, a coordinator, and relay fallback. Please review the ArnMesh Privacy Policy before using it.

Our approach

Arn1ko Studio apps are designed to keep data collection minimal. The apps are not built around advertising profiles, tracking SDKs, or selling user data. Most app settings and app state are stored locally on your device.

Information the website may process

The Arn1ko Studio website is a static project site. It does not include a user account system, custom analytics, advertising pixels, or contact forms. If the site is hosted by a third-party platform such as GitHub Pages, that platform may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, request time, and pages requested according to its own policies.

Information apps may store locally

Apps may store local settings such as selected modes, app preferences, saved profiles, interface language, theme, feature toggles, and recent app state. This information is normally kept on the device and can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

App-specific notes

ArnEQ may store sound presets, equalizer values, bass boost strength, effect toggles, language, theme, and background mode state locally. It uses Android audio and notification features to provide sound effects and background controls.

Arn Net Scanner may process local network information such as device addresses, hostnames, and scan results so it can show devices on the local network. The scanning workflow is intended to run locally on the device.

ArnProxy may store proxy profile settings locally. If you use a proxy server, the proxy provider or server operator may be able to see connection metadata or traffic depending on the protocol and encryption used. Arn1ko Studio does not control third-party proxy services configured by the user.

Arn dBA Meter may use microphone access to estimate ambient sound levels. Audio is intended to be processed locally for measurement and not uploaded by the core app workflow.

Permissions

Apps may request permissions only when needed for their features. For example, ArnEQ may request audio and notification-related permissions, Arn Net Scanner may require network access, ArnProxy may use network access, and Arn dBA Meter may request microphone access for sound level measurement.

Downloads and external services

The website may link to external download pages, GitHub Releases, app stores, social pages, or support email. Those services are operated by third parties and may process data according to their own privacy policies. Always download apps from official Arn1ko Studio links to reduce the risk of modified files.

Data sharing

Arn1ko Studio does not sell personal data. The apps do not share personal data with the developer for advertising or analytics purposes. Data may still be processed by operating systems, hosting providers, app stores, GitHub, email providers, or other third-party services you use while downloading, installing, or contacting support.

Security and user responsibility

Arn1ko Studio aims to avoid unnecessary data collection and to keep app behavior transparent. However, no software, device, network, or download channel can be guaranteed to be completely risk-free. To the maximum extent permitted by law, Arn1ko Studio is not responsible for data loss, disclosure, compromise, or damage caused by user configuration, third-party services, device malware, unofficial or modified builds, shared files, insecure networks, operating system behavior, or events outside Arn1ko Studio's control.

Children

Arn1ko Studio apps are general utility apps and are not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when app features, permissions, hosting, or distribution requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page shows the latest version.
