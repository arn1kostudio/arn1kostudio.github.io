Privacy Policy

Arn1ko Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to Android apps developed by Arn1ko Studio, including ArnEQ, Arn Net Scanner, ArnProxy, and Arn dBA Meter. The goal of these apps is to provide practical utilities while keeping user data handling clear and minimal.

Data collection

Arn1ko Studio apps are designed to avoid collecting personal data for advertising, analytics, account tracking, or cloud profiling. Unless a specific app feature clearly says otherwise, app settings and app state are stored locally on the user's device.

Data sharing

Arn1ko Studio does not sell personal data. The apps do not share personal data with the developer for advertising or analytics purposes.

Local storage

Apps may store local settings such as selected modes, app preferences, saved profiles, interface language, theme, feature toggles, and recent app state. This data is stored on the device and can usually be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

App-specific notes

ArnEQ may store sound presets, equalizer values, bass boost strength, effect toggles, language, theme, and background mode state locally. It uses Android audio and notification permissions to provide sound effects and background controls.

Arn Net Scanner may process local network information to discover devices and show scan results. The core scanning workflow is intended to run locally on the device.

ArnProxy may store proxy profile settings locally. Network behavior depends on the proxy configuration selected by the user.

Arn dBA Meter may use microphone access to estimate sound levels. Audio is intended to be processed locally for measurement and not uploaded by the core app workflow.

Permissions

Apps may request permissions only when needed for their features. For example, ArnEQ may request audio settings and notification permissions, Arn Net Scanner may require network-related access, ArnProxy may use network access, and Arn dBA Meter may request microphone access for sound level measurement.

Internet access

Some apps may use internet or network access for their core utility purpose, such as network scanning, proxy workflows, external links, or optional update/configuration checks. This does not mean the apps are collecting personal data for advertising or analytics.

Children

Arn1ko Studio apps are general utility apps and are not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when app features, permissions, or distribution requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page shows the latest version.
