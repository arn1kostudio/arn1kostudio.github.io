Arn Net Scanner

Android utility for scanning a local network, discovering devices, and checking basic connection details in a clean interface.

In development Privacy policy

Purpose

Arn Net Scanner is made for quick local-network discovery. It helps a user understand what devices are visible on the same network.

Expected features

  • Local network scan
  • Device discovery
  • IP and basic network information
  • Simple scan status view
  • Designed for troubleshooting home or small office networks

Privacy

Scan results are intended to be processed locally on the device. The app should not require user accounts, advertising SDKs, or cloud sync for its core scanning workflow.

Status

In development by Arn1ko Studio. Features and release details may change before public distribution.
