Arn dBA Meter

Android dBA meter for monitoring ambient sound levels and changes in the acoustic environment with clear visual feedback.

In development

Purpose

Arn dBA Meter is built to show ambient sound level information in dBA-style readings and help users notice changes in their acoustic environment.

Expected features

  • Sound level monitoring
  • dBA-style visual feedback
  • Status and threshold-oriented UI
  • Foreground monitoring workflow
  • Designed for quick environmental awareness

Status

In development by Arn1ko Studio. Features and release details may change before public distribution.
