Sound utility

ArnEQ

Equalizer, bass boost, sound effects, ready-made presets, background mode, and quick widget controls for Android.

Privacy policy View screenshots

Purpose

ArnEQ helps users tune everyday audio with simple presets and manual equalizer controls. It is designed as a practical Android sound utility, not a studio-grade DSP.

Core features

  • 5-band equalizer
  • Flat, Hip-Hop, Rock, Pop, Jazz, Bass+, Vocal, and Lo-Fi presets
  • Bass Boost strength control
  • Surround, Normalize, and Reverb effects where supported
  • Background mode with persistent notification
  • Home screen widget for quick start and stop

Android notes

Some effects depend on the Android device, firmware, and audio effect implementation. ArnEQ uses Android system audio effects and applies them in best-effort mode.

Privacy

ArnEQ stores selected sound profile, EQ values, language, theme, and effect toggles locally on the device. It does not use ads, analytics, accounts, or cloud sync.
